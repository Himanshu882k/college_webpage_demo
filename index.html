About Us

Pillai HOC College of Arts, Science and Commerce, Rasayani was established in the year 2008 by Dr. K. M. Vasudevan Pillai, Chairman and CEO, MES whose vision was to provide students of Rasayani with quality education in a high-tech atmosphere. It is located in a picturesque location of a 35 acre campus of green environment where learning can be, indeed a pleasure. It started off with just a handful of students, but today it has grown to a full-fledged Bachelor's College with ten disciplines, 1.e. B.A(Specialization-English, History, Economics), B.Com. (Regular), B.Com (Accounting & Finance), B.M.S., B.A. (Mass Media & Communication), B.Sc. (Computer Science) and B.Sc. (Information Technology), B.Sc. (Specialization-Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology), Master of Commerce in Accountancy (M.Com.), Master of Science in Information Technology (M.Sc.I.T.).
